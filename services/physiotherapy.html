Physiotherapy

Physiotherapy combines in-depth knowledge of how the body works with specialized hands-on- clinical assessment skills performed by a registered Physiotherapist They diagnose and treat symptoms of illness injury or disability. The physiotherapist’s goal is to provide individual treatment to restore maintain and maximize your overall well-being. The assessment may consist of functional movement, balance, strength endurance testing.

What is a chiropractic adjustment? Is it safe?

An adjustment is the main method of treatment used by your chiropractor. It is a specialized gentle, controlled and safe pressure applied to your spine to restore the proper motion and position of your vertebrae. It is a precise procedure that your highly skilled chiropractor has learned through education and perfected over years in clinical practice.

Benefits of Physiotherapy

Provide and include decreasing pain, improving joint mobility increasing strength and coordination and improve cardiovascular function. Physiotherapy aims to restore proper function to the body, and education for injury prevention is always key.

Everyone can benefit from physiotherapy whether you are living with a chronic illness, recovering from a work injury or suffering after playing that weekend sports game.
